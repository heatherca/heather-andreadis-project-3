Nonna's Lasanga

How to play

You have come over to Nonna's house for your regular Sunday dinner. She is so excited to see you that she's made your favourite, her homemade extra-cheesey lasagna. Prove your love for Nonna by eating as much lasagna as you possibly can in the time provided. To eat lasagna simply press the begin button then click or tap on the lasagna slices until they disapear.
